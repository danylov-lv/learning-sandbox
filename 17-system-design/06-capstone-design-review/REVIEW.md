# Self-critique

Fill in honestly. This is graded on whether you name real weaknesses with
real evidence, not on whether the design looks good — a self-critique
that just restates a strength as a weakness ("it's almost too scalable")
does not count as answered.

For each weakness: what specifically is weak, why you believe it is
weak, and — this is the part people skip — what evidence, if you saw it
in production, would change your mind (either confirm it is worse than
you thought, or convince you it was never a real problem).
